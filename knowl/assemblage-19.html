Laws of Exponents

  1. \( a^m\cdot a^n = a^{m+n} \)

    1. \(\dfrac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m-n} \hphantom{blank}m\gt n\)

    2. \(\displaystyle{\frac{a^m}{a^n}=\frac{1}{a^{n-m}} \hphantom{blank}m\lt n}\)

  3. \( \left(a^m\right)^n=a^{m+n} \)

  4. \( (ab)^n=a^n b^n \)

  5. \( \displaystyle{\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^n = \frac{a^n}{b^n} } \)
