Example1.2.31

Evaluate the function \(f(x)=4x^2 − x + 5\) for the following expressions.

  1. \(x = 2h\)
  2. \(x = a + 3\)
Solution

  1.  

    \begin{align*} f(\alert{2h}) &= 4(\alert{2h})^2−(\alert{2h}) + 5\\&= 4(4h^2)−2h+5\\&= 16h^2 − 2h + 5\\ \end{align*}

  2.  

    \begin{align*} f(\alert{a+3}) &= 4(\alert{a+3})^2−(\alert{a+3})+5\\&= 4(a^2+6a+9)−a-3+5\\&= 4a^2+24a+36 − a + 2\\&= 4a^2+23a + 38\\ \end{align*}
