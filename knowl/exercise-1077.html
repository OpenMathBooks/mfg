According to the theory of relativity, the mass of an object traveling at velocity \(v\) is given by the function \begin{equation*}m=\dfrac{M}{\sqrt{1-\frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \end{equation*} where \(M\) is the mass of the object at rest and \(c\) is the speed of light. Find the mass of a man traveling at a velocity of \(0.7c\) if his rest mass is \(80\) kilograms.

Answer

\(112\) kg
