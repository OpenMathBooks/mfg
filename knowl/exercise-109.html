The figure shows a graph of \(f (x) = \sqrt{x} − 2\), for \(x\gt 0\). Solve the following:

  1. \(\sqrt{x} − 2=1.5\)

  2. \(\sqrt{x} − 2=2.25\)

  3. \(\sqrt{x} − 2\lt 1\)

  4. \(\sqrt{x} − 2\gt -0.25\)

Answer

  1. \(x\approx 12\)

  2. \(x\approx 18\)

  3. \(x \lt 9\)

  4. \(x\gt 3\)
