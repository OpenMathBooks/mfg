The figure shows a graph of \(w(t) = -10(t+1)^3+10 \). Solve the following:

  1. \(-10(t+1)^3+10=100\)

  2. \(-10(t+1)^3+10=-140\)

  3. \(-10(t+1)^3+10\gt 50\)

  4. \(-20\lt -10(t+1)^3+10\lt 40\)

Answer

  1. \(t\approx -3.1\)

  2. \(t\approx 1.5\)

  3. \(t \lt 0.8\)

  4. \(-2.4\lt t\lt 0.4\)
