\(y = x^2 − 3x − 18\)

(For parts (c) and (d), it may be helpful to graph \(Y_2 = −8\) as well.)

  1. \(x^2 − 3x − 18\gt 0\)

  2. \(x^2 − 3x − 18\lt 0\)

  3. \(x^2 − 3x − 18\le -8\)

  4. \(x^2 − 3x − 18\ge -8\)

Answer

  1. \((−\infty, −3) \cup (6, \infty)\)

  2. \((-3,6) \)

  3. \([-2,5] \)

  4. \((−\infty, −2] \cup [5, \infty)\)
