\(y = 16 - x^2\)

(For parts (c) and (d), it may be helpful to graph \(Y_2 = 7\) as well.)

  1. \( 16 - x^2\gt 0\)

  2. \( 16 - x^2\lt 0\)

  3. \( 16 - x^2\le 7\)

  4. \( 16 - x^2\ge 7\)

Answer

  1. \((-4,4) \)

  2. \((−\infty, −4) \cup (4, \infty)\)

  3. \((−\infty, −3] \cup [3, \infty)\)

  4. \([-3,3] \)
