7

  1. Find \(F(−3)\), \(F(−2)\), and \(F(2)\).

  2. For what value(s) of \(s\) is \(F(s) = −1\)?

  3. Find the maximum and minimum values of \(F(s)\).

  4. For what value(s) of \(s\) does \(F\) take on its maximum and minimum values?

Answer

  1. \(2, 2, 1\)

  2. \(−6 \le s \/t −4\) or \(0\le s\lt 2\)

  3. Max: \(2\); Min: \(−1\)

  4. Max for \(−3\le s\lt −1\) or \(3\le s\lt 5\); Min for \(−6\le s\lt −4\) or \(0\le s\lt 2\)
