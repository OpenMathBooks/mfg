The figure shows a graph of \(M = g(q)\).

  1. Find all values of \(q\) for which

    1. \(g(q) = 0\)

    2. \(g(q) = 16\)

    3. \(g(q)\lt 6\)

  2. For what values of \(q\) is \(g(q)\) increasing?

Answer

    1. \(−2, 2\)

    2. \(−2.8, 0, 2.8\)

    3. \(−2.5\lt q\lt−1.25\) or \(1.25\lt q\lt 2.5\)

  2. \(−2 \lt q\lt 0\) or \(q\gt 2\)
