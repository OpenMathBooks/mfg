Exercise1.79

  1. Determine which of the points \((0, 400)\text{,}\) \((400, 200)\text{,}\) \((500, 0)\text{,}\) and \((500, 400)\) represent feasible solutions for the TrailGear problem.

  2. Find the values of the objective function \(P = 8x + 10y\) at the feasible solutions in part (a).

Answer

  1. \((0, 400)\text{,}\) \((400, 200)\text{,}\) and \((500, 0)\)

  2. At \((0, 400)\text{,}\) \(P = 4000\text{;}\) at \((400, 200)\text{,}\) \(P = 5200\text{;}\) at \((500, 0)\text{,}\) \(P = 4000\)
