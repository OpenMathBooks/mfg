\(F(x) = 0.5x^3 − 4x\)

  1. Estimate the coordinates of the turning points of the graph, that is, where the graph changes from increasing to decreasing or vice versa.

  2. Write an equation of the form \(F(a) = b\) for each turning point.

Answer

  1. \((−1.6, 4.352), (1.6, −4.352)\)

  2. \(F(−1.6) = 4.352\); \(F(1.6) = −4.352\)
