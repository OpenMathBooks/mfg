Exercise15

  1. Write an equation for a parabola with vertex at \((0,-3)\) and one of its \(x\)-intercepts at \((2,0) \).

  2. Write an equation for a parabola with vertex at \((0,-3)\) and no \(x\)-intercepts at \((2,0) \).

Answer

  1. \(y= \dfrac{3}{4}x^2-3 \)

  2. \(y=ax^2-3 \) for any \(a\lt 0\)
