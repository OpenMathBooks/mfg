Exercise12

A moving ship creates waves that impede its own speed. The function \(v = f (L) = 1.3\sqrt{L}\) gives the ship's maximum speed in knots in terms of its length, \(L\), in feet.

  1. Evaluate \(f(400)\) and explain its meaning in this context.

  2. Find the length needed for a maximum speed of \(35\) knots by substituting \(v = 35\) in the formula and solving for \(L\).

  3. Find a formula for the inverse function.

  4. Write your answer to part (b) with inverse function notation.
