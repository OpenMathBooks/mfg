Exercise19

During a flu epidemic in a small town, health officials estimate that the number of people infected \(t\) days after the first case was discovered is given by \begin{equation*}I (t) = 50 t^{3/5}\end{equation*}

  1. Make a table of values for \(I (t)\) on the domain \(0\le t\le 20\). What is the range of the function on that domain?

  2. How long will it be before \(300\) people are ill?

  3. Graph the function \(I(t)\) and verify your answer to part (b) on your graph.

Answer

  1. \(t\) \(5\) \(10\) \(15\) \(20\)
    \(I(t)\) \(131 \) \(199 \) \(254 \) \(302 \)
    Range: \([0, 302]\)

  2. \(\approx 19.812\) or about \(20\) days
