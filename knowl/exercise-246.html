A temporary typist's paycheck (before deductions) is given, in dollars, by \(S = 8t\), where \(t\) is the number of hours she worked.

  1. Make a table of values for the function.
    \(t\) \(4\) \(8\) \(20\) \(40\)
    \(S\) \(\hphantom{0000} \) \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\) \(\hphantom{0000}\)

  2. Graph the function.

  3. Using two points on the graph, compute the slope \(\frac{\Delta S}{\Delta t}\), including units.

  4. What does the slope tell us about the typist's paycheck?

Answer

  1. \(t\) \(4\) \(8\) \(20\) \(40\)
    \(S\) \(32 \) \(64\) \(160\) \(320\)

  3. 8 dollars/hour

  4. The typist is paid \(\$8\) per hour.
