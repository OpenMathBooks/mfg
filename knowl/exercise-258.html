The table shows the amount of ammonium chloride salt, in grams, that can be dissolved in 100 grams of water at different temperatures.

  1. If you plot the data, will the points lie on a straight line? Why or why not?

  2. Calculate the rate of change of salt dissolved with respect to temperature.

Answer

  1. Yes, the slope between any two points is \(\frac{1}{2}\).

  2. \(0.5\) grams of salt per degree Celsius
