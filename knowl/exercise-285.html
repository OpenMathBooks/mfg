Exercise48

Jenni lives in the San Fernando Valley, where it is hot during summer days but cools down at night. Jenni the air conditioner as little as possible. The function \(T(h)\) approximates the temperature in Jenni's house \(h\) hours after midnight. \begin{equation*} T(h) = \begin{cases} 65 \amp 0\le h \lt 8\\ 65+5h \amp 8\le h\lt 14\\ 95-\dfrac{40}{h} \amp 14\le h\lt 16\\ 75 \amp 16\le h \lt 20\\ 75-2h \amp 20\le h\lt 24 \end{cases} \end{equation*}

  1. Graph \(T(h)\). Describe what you think is happening during each piece of the graph.

  2. Find two times when the temperature inside the house is \(85\degree\) Fahrenheit.
