  1. Write an expression for the area of the square in the figure.

  2. Express the area as a polynomial.

  3. Divide the square into four pieces whose areas are given by the terms of your answer to part (b).

Answer

  1. \(A=(x+y)^2 \)

  2. \(A=x^2+2xy+y^2\)

  3. \(x^2\), \(xy\), \(xy\), \(y^2\)
