A spring is suspended from the ceiling. The table shows the length of the spring in centimeters as it is stretched by hanging various weights from it.

  1. Plot the data on graph paper and draw a straight line through the points. Estimate the \(y\)-intercept of your graph.

  2. Find an equation for the line.

  3. If the spring is stretched to \(27.56\) cm, how heavy is the attached weight?

Answer

  2. \(y = 0.12x + 25.4\)

  3. \(18\) kg
