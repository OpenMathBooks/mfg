Five pounds of body fat is equivalent to \(16,000\) calories. Carol can burn \(600\) calories per hour bicycling and \(400\) calories per hour swimming.

  1. How many calories will Carol burn in \(\) hours of cycling? How many calories will she burn in \(y\) hours of swimming?

  2. Write an equation in general form that relates the number of hours, \(x\), of cycling and the number of hours, \(y\), of swimming Carol needs to perform in order to lose \(5\) pounds.

  3. Find the intercepts and sketch the graph.

  4. What do the intercepts tell us about Carol's exercise program?
