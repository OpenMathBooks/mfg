Exercise50

  1. Write the piecewise definition for \(\abs{3x − 6}\).

  2. Use your answer to part (a) to write two inequalities that together are equivalent to \(\abs{3x − 6}\gt 9\).

  3. Solve the inequalities in part (b) and check that the solutions agree with the solutions of \(\abs{3x − 6}\gt 9\).

  4. Show that \(\abs{3x − 6}\gt 9\) is equivalent to the compound inequality \(3x − 6\lt -9~\) or \(~3x-6\gt 9\).
