Exercise52

  1. Write the piecewise definition for \(\abs{2x+5}\).

  2. Use your answer to part (a) to write two inequalities that together are equivalent to \(\abs{2x+5}\lt 7\).

  3. Solve the inequalities in part (b) and check that the solutions agree with the solutions of \(\abs{2x+5}\lt 7\).

  4. Show that \(\abs{2x+5}\lt 7\) is equivalent to the compound inequality \(-7\lt 2x+5\lt 7\).
