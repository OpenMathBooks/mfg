Exercise21

In New York City, taxi cabs charge \(\$2.50\) for distances up to \(\frac{1}{3} \) mile, plus \(\$0.40\) for each additional \(\frac{1}{5} \) mile or portion thereof. (Source: www.visitnyc.com)

  1. Sketch a graph of \(F(d)\), which gives taxi fare as a function of distance traveled, on the domain \(0\lt d \le 1\).

  2. State the range of \(F(d)\) on that domain.

  3. How much will it cost Renee to travel by taxi from Columbia University to Rockefeller Center, a distance of \(5.7\) miles?

Answer

  2. Range: \(2.50, 2.90, 3.30, 3.70, 4.10\)

  3. \(\$13.30\)
