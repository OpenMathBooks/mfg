The temperature of an automobile engine is \(9\degree\) Celsius when the engine is started and \(51\degree\)C \(7\) minutes later. Use a linear model to predict the engine temperature for both \(2\) minutes and \(2\) hours after it started. Are your predictions reasonable?

Answer

2 min: \(21\degree\)C; 2 hr: \(729\degree\)C; The estimate at 2 minutes is reasonable; the estimate at 2 hours is not reasonable.
