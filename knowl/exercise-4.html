Use the following steps to graph the solutions of the system \begin{align*} x + y \amp\le 12 \\ 3x − 4y \amp\le 8 \end{align*}

  1. Graph the boundary line \(x + y = 12\text{.}\)

  2. Lightly shade the solutions of the inequality \(x + y \le 12\text{.}\)

  3. Graph the boundary line \(3x − 4y = 8\text{.}\)

  4. Lightly shade the solutions of \(3x − 4y \le 8\text{.}\)

  5. Shade the intersection of the two solutions sets.
