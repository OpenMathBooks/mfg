Exercise59

The domain of \(f\) is \((0,+\infty)\) and the range is \((0, 1)\).

  1. \(y=5f(x) \)

  2. \(y=3f(x+2) \)

  3. \(y=2f(x-3)+2 \)

Answer

  1. Domain: \((0,\infty)\); Range: \((0,5)\)

  2. Domain: \((-2,\infty)\); Range: \((0,3)\)

  3. Domain: \((3,\infty)\); Range: \((2,4)\)
