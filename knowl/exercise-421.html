  1. Graph \(f (x) = x^2\) and \(g(x) = x^3\) on the domain \([0, 1]\) and state the range of each function. On the interval \((0, 1)\), which is greater, \(f (x)\) or \(g(x)\)?

  2. Graph \(f (x) = x^2\) and \(g(x) = x^3\) on the domain \([1, 10]\) and state the range of each function. On the interval \((1, 100)\), which is greater, \(f(x)\) or \(g(x)\)?

Answer

  1. \(f(x)\)

  2. \(g(x) \)
