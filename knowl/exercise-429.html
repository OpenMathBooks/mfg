\(y=\dfrac{1}{x^2} \)

  1. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{x^2} = 0.03\)

  2. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{x^2} = 6.25\)

  3. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{x^2}\gt 0.16 \)

  4. Solve \(\dfrac{1}{x^2}\le 4\)

Answer

  1. \(x\approx\pm 5.8 \)

  2. \(x = \pm 0.4\)

  3. \(-2.5\lt x \lt 0\) or \(0\lt x\lt 2.5\)

  4. \(x\le -0.5\) or \(x\ge 0.5\)
