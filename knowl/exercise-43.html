If raw meat is allowed to thaw at \(50\degree\)F, Salmonella grows at a rate of \(9\%\) per hour.

  1. Write a formula for the amount of Salmonella present after \(t\) hours, if the initial amount is \(50\).

  2. Health officials advise that the amount of Salmonella initially present in meat should not be allowed to increase by more than \(50\%\). How long can meat be left to thaw at \(50\degree\)F?

Answer

  1. \(S (t) = S_0(1.09)^t\)

  2. \(4.7\) hours
