You want to retire with a nest egg of one million dollars. You plan to make fixed monthly payments of \(\$1000\) into a savings account until then. How long will you need to make payments if the account earns \(6\%\) interest compounded monthly? What if the annual interest rate is \(5\%\)?

Answer

\(\approx 27\) years; \(\approx 29\) years
