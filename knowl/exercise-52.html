The weight, \(w\), of a bronze statue varies directly with the cube of its height, \(h\).

  1. If you double the height of the statue, what happens to its weight?

  2. If you make the statue one-fourth as tall, what happens to its weight?

  3. If you increase the height of the statue by \(50\%\), what happens to its weight?
