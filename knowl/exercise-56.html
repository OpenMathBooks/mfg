Exercise51

A fireworks rocket is fired from ground level. Its height in feet \(t\) seconds after launch is given by \(h = 320t − 16t^2\). During what time interval is the rocket higher than \(1024\) feet?

Answer

  1. \(320t − 16t^2 \gt 1024\); \(4 \lt t \lt 16\): Between \(4\) and \(16\) seconds
