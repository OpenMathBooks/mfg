Exercise48

A copy of Time magazine cost \(\$1.50\) in \(1981\). In \(1988\), the cover price was \(\$2.00\).

  1. What is \(P_0\) if \(t = 0\) in \(1981\)?

  2. Use the price in \(1988\) to find the growth factor \(e^k\).

  3. Find a growth law of the form \(P(t) = P_0e^{kt}\) for the price of Time.

  4. In \(1999\), a copy of Time cost \($3.50\). Did the price of the magazine continue to grow at the same rate from \(1981\) to \(1999\)?
