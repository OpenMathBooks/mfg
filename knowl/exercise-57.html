Exercise49

“The total time for a black hole to evaporate away is proportional to the cube of its initial mass. For a solar-mass hole, the lifetime is an unobservably long \(10^{64}\) years.”

  1. The solar mass is given in Problem 47. Write a formula for the lifetime, \(L\), of a black hole as a function of its mass, \(m\).

  2. The present age of the universe is about \(10^{10}\) years. What would be the mass of a black hole as old as the universe?

Answer

  1. \(L = (1.25\times 10{−27})m^3\)

  2. \(2\times 10^{12}\) kg
