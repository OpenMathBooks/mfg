  1. Write the piecewise definition for \(\abs{3x − 6}\).

  2. Use your answer to part (a) to write two inequalities that together are equivalent to \(\abs{3x − 6}\lt 9\).

  3. Solve the inequalities in part (b) and check that the solutions agree with the solutions of \(\abs{3x − 6}\lt 9\).

  4. Show that \(\abs{3x − 6}\lt 9\) is equivalent to the compound inequality \(−9\lt 3x − 6\lt 9\).

Answer

  1. \( \abs{3x-6} = \begin{cases} -(3x-6) \amp \text{if } x\lt 2 \\ 3x-6 \amp \text{if } x\ge 2 \end{cases} \)

  2. \(−(3x − 6)\le 9\), \(~3x − 6\lt 9\)

  3. \(−1\lt x\lt 5\)

  4. The solutions are the same.
