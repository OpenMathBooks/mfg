Exercise23

  1. Find the inverse of the function \(f (x) = 100 − 4^{x+2}\).

  2. Show that \(f^{−1}\) undoes the effect of \(f\) on \(x = 1\).

  3. Show that \(f\) undoes the effect of \(f^{−1}\) on \(x = 84\).

Answer

  1. \(f^{-1}(x)= \log_4 (100 − x) − 2 \)

  2. \(f^{−1} (f(1)) = f^{−1}(36)=\log_4(64)-2=1 \)

  3. \(f\left(f^{−1} (84)\right)= f (0) = 100 − 4^2 = 84\)
