Use row operations to form an equivalent matrix with the given elements: \begin{equation*} \left[ \begin{array}{@{}cc|c@{}} -1 \amp -3 \amp 7 \\ -2 \amp 4 \amp -6\\ \end{array}\right] \longrightarrow \left[ \begin{array}{@{}cc|c@{}} -1 \amp -3 \amp 7 \\ 0 \amp \text{?} \amp \text{?} \\ \end{array}\right] \end{equation*}

Answer

\(2(\text{row }1)+\text{row }2: \left[ \begin{array}{@{}cc|c@{}} -1 \amp -3 \amp 7 \\ 0 \amp -2 \amp 8 \\ \end{array}\right] \)
