Exercise23

A proportion is an equation in which each side is a ratio: \(\dfrac{a}{b} = \dfrac{c}{d} \text{.}\) Show that this equation may be rewritten as \(ad = bc\text{.}\)

Answer

Multiply both sides of the equation by \(bd\) and simplify. \begin{equation*}\frac{a}{b}\cdot \frac{bc}{1} = \frac{c}{d} \cdot \frac{bc}{1}, \text{ so } ac=bd\end{equation*}
