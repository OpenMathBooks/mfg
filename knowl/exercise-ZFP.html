Graph the function \begin{equation*}f(x) = (x − 3)(2x + 3)\end{equation*} on a calculator, and use your graph to solve the equation \(f(x) = 0\). (Use \(\text{Xmin} = −9.4\), \(\text{Xmax} = 9.4\).) Check your answer with the zero-factor principle.

Answer

\(x=-\frac{3}{2} \), \(~x=3\)
