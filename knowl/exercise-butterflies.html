%\(3600\)

  1. What is the decay factor in the annual butterfly count?

  2. Write a formula for \(B(t)\), the number of butterflies \(t\) years after 1998.

  3. Complete the table and sketch a graph of \(B(t)\).

Answer

  1. \(0.82\)

  2. \(B(t) = 3600\cdot 0.82^t\)

  3. \(t\) \(0\) \(2\) \(4\) \(6\) \(8\) \(10\)
