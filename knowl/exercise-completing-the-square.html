Complete the square by adding an appropriate constant; write the result as the square of a binomial.

  1. \(x^2 − 18x{} + {}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}}{}= (x{}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}} )^2\)
  2. \(x^2 +9x{} + {}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}}{}= (x{}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}} )^2\)

Hint

For part (a): \(p=\frac{1}{2}(-18)={}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}}, p^2={}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}}\)

For part (b): \(p=\frac{1}{2}(9)={}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}}, p^2={}\underline{\hspace{2.727272727272727em}}\)

 Answer

  1. \(x^2 − 18x + 81 = (x − 9)^2\)

  2. \(x^2+9x+\frac{81}{4}=\left(x+\frac{9}{2}\right)^2 \)
