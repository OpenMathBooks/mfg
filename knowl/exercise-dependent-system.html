Exercise1.19

  1. Use the method of elimination to solve the system \begin{align*} 3x − 4 \amp = y\\ 2y + 8 \amp = 6x \end{align*}

  2. Verify that both equations have the same graph.

Answer

  1. Elimination results in \(0x + 0y = 0\text{.}\)

  2. Both equations have slope-intercept form \(y = 3x − 4\text{.}\)
