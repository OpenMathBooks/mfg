Exercise1.13
  1. Find the inverse function for \(f(x) = 2 \log(x + 1)\).
  2. Graph \(f\) and \(f^{−1}\) in the window \begin{align} \text{Xmin} \amp = −6 \amp\amp \text{Xmax} = 6\\ \text{Ymin} \amp = −4 \amp\amp \text{Ymax} = 4 \end{align}
  3. State the domain and range of \(f\) and \(f^{−1}\).
Answer

  1. \(f^{−1}(x) = 10^{x/2} − 1 \)

  3. Domain of \(f\): \((−1,\infty)\), Range of \(f\): all real numbers, Domain of \(f^{−1}\): all real numbers, Range \(f^{-1}\): \((−1, \infty)\)
