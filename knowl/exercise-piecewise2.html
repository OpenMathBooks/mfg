Exercise 2.46
  1. Use your calculator to graph \(g(x) =\abs{x−3}\) and \(h(x) =\abs{x} + \abs{−3}\). Are the graphs the same?
  2. Explain why the functions \(f (x) =\abs{x + k}\) and \(g(x) =\abs{x} + \abs{k}\) are not the same if \(k\ne 0\).
Answer

  1. No

  2. Because \(\abs{x + k}\ne \abs{x} + \abs{k}\) when \(x\) and \(k\) have opposite signs
