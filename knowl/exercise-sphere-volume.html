Exercise1.27

The volume of a sphere of radius \(r\) centimeters is given by \begin{equation*} V = V(r) = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 \end{equation*} Evaluate \(V(10)\) and explain what it means.

Answer

\(V(10) = 4000\pi/3\approx 4188.79 \text{ cm}^3\) is the volume of a sphere whose radius is \(10\) cm.
