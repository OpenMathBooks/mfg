  1. Write a piecewise formula for the water bill, \(B(w)\text{,}\) as a function of the amount of water used, \(w\text{,}\) in HCF.

  2. Graph the function \(B\text{.}\)

Answer

  1. \(B(w) = \begin{cases} 30 + 2w \amp 0 \le w \le 50\\ 50 + 3w \amp w \gt 50\\ \end{cases}\)
