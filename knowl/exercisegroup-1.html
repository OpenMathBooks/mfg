Complete the square and write the result as the square of a binomial.

1

  1. \(x^2+8x\)

  2. \(x^2-7x\)

  3. \(x^2+\frac{3}{2}x \)

  4. \(x^2-\frac{4}{5}x \)

Answer

  1. \((x+4)^2 \)

  2. \(\left(x-\dfrac{7}{2} \right)^2 \)

  3. \(\left(x+\dfrac{3}{4} \right)^2 \)

  4. \(\left(x-\dfrac{2}{5} \right)^2 \)
