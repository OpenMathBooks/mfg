Write each expression as a power function using negative exponents.

17

  1. \(F(r)=\dfrac{3}{r^4} \)
  2. \(G(w)=\dfrac{2}{5w^3} \)
  3. \(H(z)=\dfrac{1}{(3z)^2} \)
Answer

  1. \(F(r)=3r^{-4} \)
  2. \(G(w)=\dfrac{2}{5}w^{-3} \)
  3. \(H(z)=\dfrac{1}{9}z^{-2} \)
