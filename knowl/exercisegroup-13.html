Exercise Group

Write each expression as a sum of terms of the form \(kx^p\).

41

  1. \(\dfrac{x}{3}+\dfrac{3}{x} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{x-6x^2}{4x^3} \)

Answer

  1. \(\dfrac{1}{3}x+3x^{-1} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{1}{4}x^{-2}-\dfrac{3}{2}x^{-1} \)
42

  1. \(\dfrac{2}{x^23}+\dfrac{x^2}{2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{5x+1}{(3x)}^2 \)

43

  1. \(\dfrac{2}{x^4}\left(\dfrac{x^2}{4}+\dfrac{x}{2}-\dfrac{1}{4} \right) \)

  2. \(\dfrac{x^2}{3}\left(\dfrac{2}{x^4}-\dfrac{1}{3x^2}+\dfrac{1}{2} \right) \)

Answer

  1. \(\dfrac{1}{2}x^{-2}+x^{-3}-\dfrac{1}{2}x^{-4} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{2}{3}x^{-2}-\dfrac{1}{9}+\dfrac{1}{6}x^{2} \)
44

  1. \(\dfrac{9}{x^3}\left(\dfrac{x^3}{3}-1-\dfrac{1}{x^3} \right) \)

  2. \(\dfrac{x^2}{2}\left(\dfrac{3}{x}-\dfrac{5}{x^3}+\dfrac{7}{x^5} \right) \)
