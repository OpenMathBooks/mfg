21

  1. \(2\sqrt[3]{x^2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{1}{4}\sqrt[4]{x} \)

Answer

  1. \(2x^{2/3} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{1}{4}x^{1/4} \)
