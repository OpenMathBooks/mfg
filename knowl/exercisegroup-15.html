For the given function, evaluate each pair of expressions. Are they equivalent?

19

\(f (x) = 3(5^x )\)

  1. \(f (a + 2)\) and \(9f (a)\)

  2. \(f (2a)\) and \(2 f (a)\)

Answer

  1. \(3(5^{a+2})\) is not equivalent to \(9\cdot 3(5^a)\).

  2. \(3(5^{2a})\) is not equivalent to \(2\cdot 3 (5^a)\).
20

\(g(x) = 1.8^x\)

  1. \(g(h + 3)\) and \(g(h) g(3)\)

  2. \(g(2h)\) and \([g(h)]^2 \)

21

\(P(t) = 8^t\)

  1. \(P(w)-P(z) \) and \(P(w-z) \)

  2. \(P(-x) \) and \(\dfrac{1}{P(x)} \)

Answer

  1. \(8^w − 8^z\) is not equivalent to \(8^{w−z}\).

  2. \(8^{−x}\) is equivalent to \(\dfrac{1}{8^x}\).
