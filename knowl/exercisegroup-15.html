Exercise Group

For Problems 7–12,

  1. Find a formula for the inverse \(f^{-1} \) of each function.

  2. Graph the function and its inverse on the same set of axes, along with the graph of \(y=x\).

7

\(f(x)=x+4 \)

Answer

  1. \(f^{−1} (x) = x − 4\)

8

\(f(x)=\dfrac{x-2}{4} \)

9

\(f(x)=x^3-1 \)

Answer

  1. \(f^{−1} (x) =\sqrt[3]{x+1} \)

10

\(f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x+2} \)

11

\(f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x}+2 \)

Answer

  1. \(f^{−1} (x) =\dfrac{1}{x-2} \)

12

\(f(x)=\sqrt[3]{x}-2 \)
