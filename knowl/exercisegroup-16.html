In Problems 1–8, use the graphs to answer the questions about the functions.

1

  1. Find \(h(−3)\), \(h(1)\), and \(h(3)\).

  2. For what value(s) of \(z\) is \(h(z) = 3\)?

  3. Find the intercepts of the graph. List the function values given by the intercepts.

  4. What is the maximum value of \(h(z)\)?

  5. For what value(s) of \(z\) does \(h\) take on its maximum value?

  6. On what intervals is the function increasing? Decreasing?

2

  4. What is the minimum value of \(G(s)\)?

  5. For what value(s) of \(s\) does \(G\) take on its minimum value?

  6. On what intervals is the function increasing? Decreasing?
