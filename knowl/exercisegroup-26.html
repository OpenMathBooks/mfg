In Problems 35–38, the graph of a function is shown. Describe each transformation of the graph; then give a formula for each in terms of the original function.

35

Answer

  1. Vertically stretched by a factor of \(3\): \(y = 3 f (x)\)

  2. Reflected about the \(x\)-axis: \(y = −f (x)\)

  3. Translated \(1\) unit right: \(y = f (x − 1)\)

  4. Translated \(4\) units up: \(y = f (x) + 4\)

Answer

  1. Reflected about the \(v\)-axis and stretched vertically by a factor of \(2\): \(T = −2h(v)\)

  2. Stretched vertically by a factor of \(3\): \(T = 3h(v)\)

  3. Translated \(3\) units up: \(T = h(v) + 3\)

  4. Translated \(3\) units left: \(T = h(v + 3)\)
