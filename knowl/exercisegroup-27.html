Answer

  1. Translated \(2\) units up: \(y = f (x) + 2\)

  2. Translated \(4\) units down: \(y = f (x) − 4\)

  3. Vertically compressed with a factor of \(\frac{1}{2} \): \(y = \frac{1}{2}f (x)\)

  4. Translated \(1\) unit right: \(y = f (x − 1)\)
