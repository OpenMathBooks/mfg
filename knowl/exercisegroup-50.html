Find the domain of each function algebraically. Then graph the function, and use the graph to help you find the range.

25

  1. \(f(x)=\dfrac{1}{(x-4)^2} \)

  2. \(h(x)=\dfrac{1}{x^2}-4 \)

Answer

  1. \(f (x)\) domain: \(x \ne 4\); Range: \((0,\infty)\)

  2. \(h (x)\) domain: \(x \ne 0\); Range: \((-4,\infty)\)

27

  1. \(G(v)=v^3+35 \)

  2. \(H(v)\) domain: all real numbers; Range: all real numbers
