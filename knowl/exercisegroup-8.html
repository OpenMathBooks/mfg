In Problems 69 and 70, we prove the quadratic formula.

69

Complete the square to find the solutions of the equation \(x^2 + bx + c = 0\). (Your answers will be expressions in \(b\) and \(c\).)

Answer

\(\dfrac{-b\pm \sqrt{b^2-4c}}{2} \)
