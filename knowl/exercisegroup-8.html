7

  1. \(2^{-1}\)

  2. \((-5)^{-2} \)

  3. \(\left(\dfrac{1}{3} \right)^{-3} \)

  4. \(\dfrac{1}{(-2)^{-4}} \)

Answer

  1. \(\dfrac{1}{2^1}=\dfrac{1}{2} \)

  2. \(\dfrac{1}{(-5)^2}=\dfrac{1}{25} \)

  3. \(3^3=27 \)

  4. \((-2)^4=16 \)
