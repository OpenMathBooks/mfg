\(f (x) = x^3\)

  1. \(f (a^2)\)

  2. \(a^3 \cdot f (a^3)\)

  3. \(f (ab)\)

  4. \(f (a + b)\)

Answer

  1. \(a^6\)

  2. \(a^{12}\)

  3. \(a^3b^3\)

  4. \(a^3 + 3a^2b + 3ab^2 + b^3 \)
