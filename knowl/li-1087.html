Item10

  • The laws of exponents are valid for all exponents \(m\) and \(n\), and for \(b\ne 0\).

    Laws of Exponents

    1. \(a^m\cdot a^n=a^{m+n}\)

    2. \(\dfrac{a^m}{a^n}=a^{m-n}\)

    3. \(\left(a^m\right)^n=a^{mn}\)

    4. \(\left(ab\right)^n=a^n b^n\)

    5. \(\left(\dfrac{a}{b}\right)^n=\dfrac{a^n}{b^n} \)
