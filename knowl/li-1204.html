Item a
Write the equation for \(f\) in the form \begin{equation*}H = 6 + 2t\end{equation*} In this equation, \(t\) is the input and \(H\) is the output. We interchange the roles of the variables by solving for \(t\) to obtain \begin{equation*}t = \frac{H − 6}{2}\end{equation*} In this equation, \(H\) is the input and \(t\) is the output. The formula for the inverse function is \(t=g(H) = \frac{H − 6}{2}\). The function \(g\) gives the number of days it will take the corn seedlings to grow to a height of \(H\) inches.
