  • Solve the equation \begin{equation*}7.5 = −\log_{10}[H^+]\end{equation*} for \([H^+]\). First, write \begin{equation*}−7.5 = \log_{10}[H^+]\end{equation*} Then convert the equation to exponential form to get \begin{equation*}[H^+] = 10^{−7.5}\approx 3.2 \times 10^{−8}\end{equation*} The hydrogen ion concentration of the water is \(3.2 \times 10^{−8}\).
