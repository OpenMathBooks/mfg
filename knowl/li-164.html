Item1

  • Compare the two expressions \(3a^2\) and \((3a)^2\text{;}\) they are not the same. In the expression \(3a^2\text{,}\) only the factor \(a\) is squared. But in \((3a)^2\text{,}\) both \(3\) and \(a\) are squared. Thus, \begin{equation*}3a^2 ~~\text{ cannot be simplified}\end{equation*} but \begin{equation*}(3a)^2 = 3^2a^2 = 9a^2\end{equation*}
