  • Studies of the cheetah have revealed variation at only \(3.2\%\) of its genes. (Other species show variation at \(10\%\) to \(43\%\) of their genes.) The population of cheetah may be less than \(5000\). Assuming the population can be maintained at its current level, how many generations will it take before the cheetah's genetic variation is reduced to \(1\%\)?
