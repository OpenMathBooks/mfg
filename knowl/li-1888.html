Item b

The equation \begin{equation*}mg=\frac{GMm}{R^2} \end{equation*} gives the force of gravity at the Earth's surface. We can use this equation to simplify the expression for \(v\): First, multiply both sides of the equation by \(\frac{R}{M}\). You now have an expression for \(\frac{GM}{R}\). Substitute this new expression into your formula for \(v\).
