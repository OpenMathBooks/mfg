Item a
The initial value of the equipment is \(V_0 = 50,000\). Every \(18\) months, the value of the equipment is multiplied by \begin{equation*}b = 1 − r = 1 − 0.50 = 0.50\end{equation*} However, because \(18\) months is \(1.5\) years, we must divide \(t\) by \(1.5\) in our formula, giving us \begin{equation*}V(t) = 50,000(0.50)^{t/1.5}\end{equation*}
