The safe dosage \(d\) satisfies \(250 \le d \le 450\), as shown in Figure 2.123. The center of this interval is \(350\), and the endpoints are each \(100\) units from the center. Thus, the safe values are within \(100\) units of \(350\), or \begin{equation*}\abs{d − 350} ≤ 100\end{equation*}
