Item a

If there is a headwind of \(x\) miles per hour, Francine's ground speed will be \(15 − x\) miles per hour. Using the fact that time \(\text{time} = \frac{\text{distance}}{\text{rate}}\), we find that the time needed for the flight will be \begin{equation*} t = f (x) = \frac{60}{15 − x} \end{equation*}
