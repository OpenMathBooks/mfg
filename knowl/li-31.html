In 1990 the median price was $92,000. Four years later, in 1994, the price had increased by \(\alert{4}(4700)=18,800\) dollars, so \begin{equation*}P=92,000+\alert{4}(4700)=110,800\end{equation*} In 1998 the price had increased by \(\alert{8}(4700)=37,600\) dollars, so \begin{equation*}P=92,000+\alert{8}(4700)-129,600\end{equation*} You can verify the price of the house in 2000 by a similar calculation.
