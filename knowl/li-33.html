  • To find the price of butter at any time after inflation began, we evaluate the function at the appropriate value of \(t\). \begin{align*} P(\alert{3}) \amp = 2(1.12)^{2(\alert{3})} \\ \amp = 2(1.12)^6 \approx 3.95 \end{align*} After \(3\) years, the price was $\(3.95\). Fifteen months is \(1.25\) years, so we evaluate \(P(1.25)\). \begin{align*} P(\alert{1.25}) \amp = 2(1.12)^{2(\alert{1.25})} \\ \amp = 2(1.12)^{2.5} \approx 2.66 \end{align*} After \(15\) months, the price of butter was $\(2.66\).
