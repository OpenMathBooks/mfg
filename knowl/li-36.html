  • Compounded Interest

    The amount \(A(t)\) accumulated (principal plus interest) in an account bearing interest compounded \(n\) times annually is \begin{equation*}A(t)=\left(1 + \dfrac{r}{n} \right)^{nt}\end{equation*} where

    \(P\) is the principal invested,
    \(r\) is the interest rate,
    \(t\) is the time period, in years.
