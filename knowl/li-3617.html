Item 2

Compare the two expressions \((3a)^2\) and \((3 + a)^2\). The fourth law of exponents applies to the product \(3a\), but not to the sum \(3+a\). Thus, \begin{equation*}(3 + a)^2 \ne 3^2 + a^2\end{equation*} In order to simplify \((3 + a)^2\), we must expand the binomial product: \begin{equation*}(3 + a)^2 = (3 + a) (3 + a) = 9 + 6a + a^2\end{equation*}
