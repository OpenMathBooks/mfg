Item b

Apply Property (2) to write the quotient as a single radical. \begin{align*} \frac{\sqrt[3]{16y^5}}{\sqrt[3]{y^2}}\amp =\sqrt[3]{\frac{16y^5}{y^2}}\amp\amp\text{Reduce.} \\ \amp = \sqrt[3]{16y^3}\amp\amp\text{Simplify: factor out perfect cubes.} \\ \amp = \sqrt[3]{8y^3}\sqrt[3]{2} \\ \amp = 2y \sqrt[3]{2} \end{align*}
