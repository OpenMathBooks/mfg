If \(C\) is negative, the signs in the factored form are opposite. Thus as a first step in factoring \(6x^2 − 5x − 4\), we could write \begin{equation*}(\hphantom{000} + \hphantom{000} ) (\hphantom{000} - \hphantom{000} ) ~~\text{ or }~~ (\hphantom{000} - \hphantom{000} ) (\hphantom{000} + \hphantom{000} )\end{equation*}
