This is a quadratic equation with no linear term, so we simplify and then isolate \(x^2\). \begin{align*} x^2+4x^2\amp =225\amp\amp\text{Combine like terms.} \\ 5x^2\amp = 225\amp\amp\text{Divide both sides by 5.} \\ x^2\amp = 45 \end{align*} Taking square roots of both sides yields \begin{equation*}x=\pm\sqrt{45}\approx\pm 6.708203932 \end{equation*}
