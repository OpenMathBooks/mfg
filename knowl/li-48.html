Item b

Choose some values of \(x\), and solve the equation for the corresponding value of \(y\) For example, if \(x=\alert{10}\) then \begin{align*} 150(10)+50y\amp=300\\ 1500+50y\amp=3000\\ 50y\amp=1500\\ y\amp=30 \end{align*} If the manager buys 10 television ads, she can also buy 30 radio ads. You can verify the other entries in the table.
