  • We want to evaluate our expression from part (a) with \(S = 350\). We substitute \(\alert{350}\) for \(S\) to find \begin{equation*}I = 200 + 0.09(\alert{350})\end{equation*} Following the order of operations, we perform the multiplication before the addition. Thus, we begin by computing \(0.09(350)\). \begin{align*} I \amp = 200 + 0.09(350)\amp\amp\text{Multiply }0.09 (350) \text{ first.} \\ \amp = 200 + 31.5\\ \amp = 231.50 \end{align*} April's income for the week is \(\$231.50\).
