  • Twice 148 million is 296 million, so we solve the equation \begin{align*} 148(1.02)^t \amp= 296\amp\amp\text{Divide both sides by }148.\\ 1.02^t \amp= 2\amp\amp\text{Take the log of both sides.}\\ t \log 1.02 \amp= \log 2\amp\amp\text{Divide both sides by log 1.02.}\\ t \amp= \frac{\log 2}{\log 1.02}\\ \amp\approx 35 \text{ years} \end{align*} It will take the population about \(35\) years to double again.
