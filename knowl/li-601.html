Item4

  • Interest Compounded Annually

    The amount \(A(t)\) accumulated (principal plus interest) in an account bearing interest compounded annually is \begin{equation*}A(t)=(1 + r)^t\end{equation*} where

    \(P\) is the principal invested,
    \(r\) is the interest rate,
    \(t\) is the time period, in years.
