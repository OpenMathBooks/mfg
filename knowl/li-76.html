  • Exponential Growth and Decay

    The function \begin{equation*}P(t) = P_0 b^t\end{equation*} models exponential growth and decay.

    \(P_0 = P(0) \) is the initial value of \(P\);

    \(b\) is the growth or decay factor.

    1. If \(b\gt 1\), then \(P(t)\) is increasing, and \(b = 1 + r\), where \(r\) represents percent increase.

    2. If \(0\lt b\lt 1\), then \(P(t)\) is decreasing, and \(b = 1 âˆ’ r\), where \(r\) represents percent decrease.
