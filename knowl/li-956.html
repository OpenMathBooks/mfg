To graph \(f\), we translate the graph of \(y = 2^x\) by \(3\) units to the right and \(4\) units down. The graph of \(f^{−1}\) looks like the graph of \(y = \log_2 x\) shown in Figure 5.2.7, but shifted \(4\) units to the left and \(3\) units up. The graphs are shown in Figure 5.2.12, along with the line \(y = x\).
