Because \(e\) is a number between \(2\) and \(3\), the graph of \(f(x) = e^x\) lies between the graphs of \(y = 2^x\) and \(y = 3^x\). Compare the tables of values (Table 1.60) and the graphs of the three functions in Figure 1.61. As with other exponential functions, the domain of the natural exponential function includes all real numbers, and its range is the set of positive numbers.
