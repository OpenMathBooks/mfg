One method for measuring the magnitude of an earthquake compares the amplitude \(A\) of its seismographic trace with the amplitude \(A_0\) of the smallest detectable earthquake. The log of their ratio is the Richter magnitude, \(M\). Thus, \begin{equation*}M=\log_{10}\left(\frac{A}{A_0} \right)\end{equation*}
