The cost, in thousands of dollars, for immunizing \(p\) percent of the residents of Emporia against a dangerous new disease is given by the function \begin{equation*}C(p)=\frac{72p}{100-p} \end{equation*}

  1. What is the domain of \(C\text{?}\)

  2. Complete the table showing the cost of immunizing various percentages of the population.

  3. Graph the function \(C\text{.}\) (Use \(\text{Xmin} = 6\text{,}\) \(\text{Xmax}= 100\text{,}\) and appropriate values of Ymin and Ymax.) What percentage of the population can be immunized if the city is able to spend \(\$108,000\text{?}\)

  4. For what values of \(p\) is the total cost more than \(\$1,728,000\text{?}\)

  5. The graph has a vertical asymptote. What is it? What is its significance in the context of this problem?
