The length of a rectangle is \(10\) inches, and its width is \(8\) inches. Suppose we increase the length of the rectangle while holding the width constant.

  1. Fill in the table.

  2. Does the perimeter vary directly with the length?

  3. Write a formula for the perimeter of the rectangle in terms of its length.

  4. Does the area vary directly with the length?

  5. Write a formula for the area of the rectangle in terms of its length.
