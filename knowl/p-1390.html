Example 6.5.1, we solved the inequality \(256t − 16t^2 /gt 800\) by comparing points on the graph of \(h = 256 − 16t^2\) with points on the line \(h = 800\). If one side of an inequality is zero, we can compare points on the graph with the line \(y = 0\), which is the \(x\)-axis.
