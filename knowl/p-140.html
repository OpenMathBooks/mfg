The cost, in thousands of dollars, for immunizing \(p\) percent of a precious ore from a mine is given by the equation \begin{equation*}C(p)=\frac{360p}{100-p} \end{equation*}

  1. What is the domain of \(C\text{?}\)

  2. Complete the table showing the cost of extracting various percentages of the ore.

  3. Graph the function \(C\text{.}\) (Use \(\text{Xmin} = 6\text{,}\) \(\text{Xmax}= 100\text{,}\) and appropriate values of Ymin and Ymax.) What percentage of the ore can be extracted if \(\$540,000\) can be spent on the extraction?

  4. For what values of \(p\) is the total cost less than \(\$1,440,000\text{?}\)

  5. The graph has a vertical asymptote. What is it? What is its significance in the context of this problem?
