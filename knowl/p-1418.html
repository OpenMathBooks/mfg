The parabola in FigureÂ 6.88a opens upward. It crosses the \(x\)-axis at two points, \(x = r_1\) and \(x = r_2\). At these points, \(y = 0\). The graph lies below the \(x\)-axis between \(r_1\) and \(r_2\), so the solutions to the inequality \(y\lt 0\) lie between \(r_1\) and \(r_2\). The graph lies above the \(x\)-axis for \(x\)-values less than \(r_1\) or greater than \(r_2\), so the solutions to the inequality \(y\gt 0\) are \(x\lt r_1\) or \(x\gt r_2\).
