The quadratic Bézier curve is defined by two endpoints, \((x_1, y_1)\) and \((x_3, y_3)\), and a control point, \((x_2, y_2)\). \begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} x \amp= f(t)=x_1\cdot(1 − t)^2+2x_2\cdot t (1 − t) + x_3\cdot t^2 \\ y \amp = g(t)= y_1\cdot(1 − t)^2 +2y_2\cdot t (1 − t) + y_3 · t^2 \end{aligned} \end{equation*} for \(0\le t\le 1\).
