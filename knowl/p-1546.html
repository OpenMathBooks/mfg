n, the middle number in a set of numbers when written in increasing order. For example, the median of \(5\), \(2\), and \(11\) is \(5\). If the set has two numbers in the middle when written in order, then the median of the set is the mean of those middle numbers. For example, the median of \(6\), \(1\), \(9\), and \(27\) is \(\frac{6+9}{2}= 7.5\).
