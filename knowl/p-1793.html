The round-trip flying time from Dallas to Phoenix and back is \begin{equation*} F(r ) =\frac{800}{r-30}+\frac{800}{r+30} \end{equation*} The LCD for these fractions is \((r − 30)(r + 30)\). Thus, \begin{align*} \frac{800}{r-30}+\frac{800}{r+30} \amp= \frac{800\alert{({r+30})}}{(r-30)\alert{({r+30})}}+\frac{800\blert{({r-30})}}{(r+30)\blert{({r-30})}} \\ \amp = \frac{(800r + 24000) + (800r − 24000)} {(r + 30)(r − 30)} \\ \amp = \frac{1600r}{r^2 − 900} \end{align*}
