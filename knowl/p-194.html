Use the following formula to answer Problems 43 and 44. If an object is thrown into the air from a height \(s_0\) above the ground with an initial velocity \(v_0\), its height \(t\) seconds later is given by the formula \begin{equation*}h=-\frac{1}{2}gt^2+v_0 t + s_0 \end{equation*} where \(g\) is a constant that measures the force of gravity.
