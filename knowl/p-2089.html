To compute the slope of the line of best fit, we first choose two points on the line. Our line appears to pass through one of the data points,\((8.8, 139.6)\text{.}\) Look for a second point on the line whose coordinates are easy to read, perhaps \((6.5,100)\text{.}\) The slope is

\begin{equation*} m = \frac{139.6 - 100}{8.8 - 6.5}= 17.2\text{ miles per gallon} \end{equation*}

According to our data, the SUV gets about 17.2 miles to the gallon.
