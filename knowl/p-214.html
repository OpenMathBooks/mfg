The admissions at a Bengals' baseball game was \(\$7.50\) for adults and \(\$4.25\) for students. The ticket office took in \(\$465.50\) for \(82\) paid admissions. How many adults and how many students attended the game?

  1. Write algebraic expressions to fill in the table.

  2. Write an equation about the number of tickets sold.

  3. Write a second equation about the revenue from the tickets.

  4. Graph both equations and solve the system.
