Consider the expression \(\left(a^4\right)^3\), the third power of \(a^4\). \begin{equation*}\left(a^4\right)^3=\left(a^4\right)\left(a^4\right)\left(a^4\right) =a^{4+4+4}=a^{12} \hphantom{blankblankblank}\text{Add exponents.}\end{equation*} We can obtain the same result by multiplying the exponents together. \begin{equation*}\left(a^4\right)^3=a^{4 \,\cdot\, 3}=a^{12}\end{equation*}
