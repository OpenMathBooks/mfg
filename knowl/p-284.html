The resistance, \(R\), of an electrical wire varies inversely with the square of its diameter, \(d\).

  1. If you replace an old wire with a new one whose diameter is half that of the old one, what happens to the resistance?

  2. If you replace an old wire with a new one whose diameter is two-thirds of the old one, what happens to the resistance?

  3. If you decrease the diameter of the wire by \(30\%\), what happens to the resistance?
