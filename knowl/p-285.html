Suppose that \(z = a + bi\) and that \(a\) and \(b\) are both real numbers.

  1. If \(a\ne 0\) and \(b=0\), then what is the slope of the segment in the complex plane joining the origin to \(z\)? What is the slope of the segment joining the origin to \(iz\)?

  2. If \(a=-0\) and \(b \ne 0\), then what is the slope of the segment in the complex plane joining the origin to \(z\)? What is the slope of the segment joining the origin to \(iz\)?

  3. What can you conclude about the angle between the two segments from parts (a) and (b)?
