The box has six sides; we must find the area of each side and add them. The top and bottom of the box each have area \(lw\), so together they contribute \(2lw\) to the surface area. The back and front of the box each have area \(lh\), so they contribute \(2lh\) to the surface area. Finally, the left and right sides of the box each have area \(wh\), so they add \(2wh\) to the surface area. Thus, the total surface area is \begin{equation*}S = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh\end{equation*}
