You plan to deposit your savings of \(\$1600\) in an account that compounds interest annually.

  1. Write a formula for the amount in your savings account after two years as a function of the interest rate, \(r\).

  2. To the nearest tenth of a percent, what interest rate will you require if you want your \(\$1600\) to grow to \(\$2000\) in two years?

  3. Use your calculator to graph the formula for the account balance. Locate the point on the graph that corresponds to the amount in part (b).
