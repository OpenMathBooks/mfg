In part (2), as you increased the value of \(n\), the other parameters in the formula stayed the same. In other words, \(A\) is a function of \(n\), given by \(A = 1000 (1 + \frac{0.08}{n} )^n\). Use your calculator to graph A on successively larger domains:

  1. \( \text{Xmin} = 0, \text{Xmax} = 12; \text{Ymin} = 1080, \text{Ymax} = 1084 \)

  2. \( \text{Xmin} = 0, \text{Xmax} = 50; \text{Ymin} = 1080, \text{Ymax} = 1084 \)

  3. \( \text{Xmin} = 0, \text{Xmax} = 365; \text{Ymin} = 1080, \text{Ymax} = 1084 \)
