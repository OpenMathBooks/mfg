For example, by applying this new version of the law for quotients, we find \begin{equation*}\frac{x^2}{x^5}= x^{2−5} = x^{−3}\end{equation*} which is consistent with our previous version of the rule, \begin{equation*}\frac{x^2}{x^5}= \frac{1}{x^{5−2}}= \frac{1}{x^3}\end{equation*}
