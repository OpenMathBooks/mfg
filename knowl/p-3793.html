The body mass index, or BMI, is one measure of a person’s physical fitness. Your body mass index is defined by \begin{equation*}BMI = wh^{−2}\end{equation*} where \(w\) is your weight in kilograms and \(h\) is your height in meters. The World Health Organization classifies a person as obese if his or her BMI is \(25\) or higher.
