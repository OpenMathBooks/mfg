The volume, \(V\), of a gas varies directly with the temperature, \(T\), and inversely with the pressure, \(P\), of the gas.

  1. If \(V = 40\) when \(T = 300\) and \(P = 30\), express the volume of the gas as a function of the temperature and pressure of the gas.

  2. Find the volume when \(T = 320\) and \(P = 40\).
