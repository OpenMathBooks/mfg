The amount \(A(t)\) accumulated (principal plus interest) in an account bearing interest compounded \(n\) times annually is \begin{equation*}A(t)=P\left(1+ \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}\end{equation*} where
\(P\) is the principal invested,
\(r\) is the interest rate,
\(t\) is the time period, in years.
