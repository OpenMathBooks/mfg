Note that we do not multiply the exponents when simplifying a product. For example, \begin{equation*}b^4 \cdot b^2 \ne b^8\end{equation*} You can check this with your calculator by choosing a value for \(b\), for instance, \(b = 3\): \begin{equation*}3^4 \cdot 3^2\ne 3^8\end{equation*}
