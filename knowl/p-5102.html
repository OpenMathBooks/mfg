Recall that the equation of a vertical line has the form \begin{equation*}x=k\end{equation*} where \(k\) is a constant, and a horizontal line has an equation of the form \begin{equation*}y=k\end{equation*} Similarly, the inequality \(x\ge k\) may represent the inequality in two variables \begin{equation*}x+0y\ge k\end{equation*} Its graph is then a region in the plane.
