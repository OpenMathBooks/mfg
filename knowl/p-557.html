Suppose the solutions of the equation \(\abs{ax+b}=c\) are \(r\) and \(s\), with \(r \lt s\). Then
  1. The solutions of \(\abs{ax+b} \lt c\) are \begin{equation*}r \lt x \lt s\end{equation*}
  2. The solutions of \(\abs{ax+b} \gt c\) are \begin{equation*}x \lt r \text{ or } x \gt s\end{equation*}
