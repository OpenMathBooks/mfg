Set the graphing window to \begin{align*} \text{Xmin} \amp = -9.4 \amp\amp \text{Xmax} = 9.4 \\ \text{Ymin} \amp = -10 \amp\amp \text{Ymax} = 10 \end{align*} and enter the two equations We can see in Figure 8.3 that the two lines intersect in the third quadrant. Use the TRACE key or the intersect feature to find the coordinates of the intersection point. Check that the point \((−2,−3)\) lies on both graphs. The solution to the system is \(x = −2\), \(y=-3\).
