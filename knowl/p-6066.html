Factor the polynomial to obtain \begin{align*} g(x) \amp= (x^2 − 4)(x^2 + 1) \\ \amp= (x − 2)(x + 2)(x^2 + 1) \end{align*} Because \(x^2 + 1\) cannot be factored in real numbers, the graph has only two \(x\)-intercepts, at \((−2, 0)\) and \((2, 0)\).
