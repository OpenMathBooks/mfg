To decide which side of the line to shade, we can solve the inequality for \(y\) in terms of \(x\text{.}\). If we obtain \begin{equation*} y \ge mx + b ~~~(\text{or }~~~y \gt mx + b) \end{equation*} then we shade the half-plane above the line. If the inequality is equivalent to \begin{equation*} y \le mx + b ~~~(\text{or }~~~y \lt mx + b) \end{equation*} then we shade the half-plane below the line.
