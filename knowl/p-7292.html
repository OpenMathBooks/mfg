Substitute \((2, −1)\) for \((x_1, y_1)\) and substitute \((4, 3)\) for \((x_2, y_2)\) in the distance formula to obtain \begin{align*} d\amp =\sqrt{(x_2 − x_1)^2 + (y_2 − y_1)^2} \\ \amp =\sqrt{(4 − 2)^2 + \left[3 − (-1)\right]^2} \\ \amp = \sqrt{4+16}=\sqrt{20}\approx 4.47 \end{align*}
