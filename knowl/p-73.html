Paragraph

  1. If \(ax^2 + bx \gt c\) for a particular value of \(x\), what can you say about the graph of \(y = ax^2 + bx − c\) at that \(x\)-value?

  2. What are the only \(x\)-values at which the graph of \(y = ax^2 + bx + c\) can change sign?

  3. Explain the difference between an open interval and a closed interval.

  4. Explain what is wrong with the following "solution" to a quadratic inequality: \(2\gt x\gt 8\).

  5. The parabola \(y = x^2 + bx + c\) has \(x\)-intercepts at \(r_1\) and \(r_2\), with \(r_1\lt r_2\). What are the solutions of the inequality \(x^2 + bx + c \gt 0\)?

  6. The parabola \(y = x^2 + bx + c\) has \(x\)-intercepts at \(r_1\) and \(r_2\), with \(r_1\lt r_2\). What are the solutions of the inequality \(x^2 + bx + c \le 0\)?

  7. The parabola \(y = −x^2 + bx + c\) has \(x\)-intercepts at \(r_1\) and \(r_2\), with \(r_1\lt r_2\). What are the solutions of the inequality \(−x^2 + bx + c \le 0\)?

  8. The parabola \(y = −x^2 + bx + c\) has \(x\)-intercepts at \(r_1\) and \(r_2\), with \(r_1\lt r_2\). What are the solutions of the inequality \(−x^2 + bx + c \gt 0\)?
