To find the coordinates of the vertices \(A\text{,}\), \(B\text{,}\), \(C\text{,}\), and \(D\text{,}\), solve simultaneously the equations of the two lines that intersect at the vertex. Thus,

for \(A\text{,}\) solve the system

\begin{align*} x \amp= 0\\ y\amp =0 \end{align*}

to find \((0, 0)\)

for \(B\text{,}\) solve the system

\begin{align*} x \amp= 0\\ x + 2y \amp= 6 \end{align*}

to find \((0, 3)\)

for \(C\text{,}\) solve the system

\begin{align*} x + 2y \amp= 6\\ x -y \amp= 2 \end{align*}

to find \(\left(\frac{10}{3},\frac{4}{3} \right)\)

for \(D\text{,}\) solve the system

\begin{align*} y \amp= 0\\ x -y \amp= 2 \end{align*}

to find \(\left(2,0 \right)\)

The vertices are the points \((0, 0)\text{,}\), \((0, 3)\text{,}\), \(\left(\frac{10}{3},\frac{4}{3} \right)\text{,}\), and \((2, 0)\text{.}\).
