Small animals such as bats cannot survive for long without eating. The graph in Figure 3.4.17 shows how the weight, \(W\), of a typical vampire bat decreases over time until its next meal, until the bat reaches the point of starvation. The curve is the graph of the function \begin{equation*}W(h) = 130.25h^{−0.126}\end{equation*} where \(h\) is the number of hours since the bat’s most recent meal. (Source: Wilkinson, 1984)
