Recall that the graph of \(y = −f (x)\) is the reflection about the \(x\)-axis of the graph of \(y = f (x)\). The graphs of \(y = 2^x\) and \(y = −2^x\) are shown in Figure 4.2.11. You may have also noticed a relationship between the graphs of \(f (x) = 2^x\) and \(g(x) = \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^x\), which are shown in Figure 4.2.12.
