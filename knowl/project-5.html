This project derives the change of base formula.

  1. Follow the steps below to calculate \(\log_8 20\).

    Step 1

    Let \(x = \log_8 20\). Write the equation in exponential form.

    Step 2

    Take the logarithm base \(10\) of both sides of your new equation.

    Step 3

    Simplify and solve for \(x\).

  2. Follow the steps in part (a) to calculate \(\log_8 5\).

  3. Use part (a) to find a formula for calculating \(\log_8 Q\), where \(Q\) is any positive number.

  4. Find a formula for calculating \(\log_b Q\), where \(b\gt 1\) and \(Q\) is any positive number.

  5. Find a formula for calculating \(\ln Q\) in terms of \(\log_{10} Q\).

  6. Find a formula for calculating \(\log_{10} Q\) in terms of \(\ln Q\).
