Remark1.3.31Using the Trace Feature

You can use the Trace feature on a graphing calculator to approximate solutions to equations. Graph the function \(f(x)\) in ExampleÂ 1.3.27 in the window

and trace along the curve to the point \((2.4680851, 15.512401)\). We are close to a solution, because the \(y\)-value is close to \(15\). Try entering \(x\)-values close to \(2.4680851\), for instance, \(x = 2.4\) and \(x = 2.5\), to find a better approximation for the solution.

We can use the intersect feature on a graphing calculator to obtain more accurate estimates for the solutions of equations. See <<appendix-b>> for details.
