Solution6.39.1

Both equations are of the form \(y = ax^2\). The graph of \(y = 2x^2\) opens upward because \(a = 2 \gt 0\), and the graph of \(y = −\frac{1}{2}x^2\) opens downward because \(a = −\frac{1}{2}\lt 0\). To make a reasonable sketch by hand, it is enough to plot a few guidepoints; the points with \(x\)-coordinates \(1\) and \(−1\) are easy to compute.
