Solution 1.4.1.1 We compute the growth rate for each strain of wheat. Growth rate is expressed as a ratio, \(\frac{\text{centimeters}}{\text{days}}\), or centimeters per day. The growth rate for Hybrid A is \[\frac{11.2 \text{ centimeters}}{14 \text{ days}}= 0.8 \text{ centimeters per day}\] and the growth rate for Hybrid B is \[\frac{13.5 \text{ centimeters}}{18 \text{ days}}= 0.75 \text{ centimeters per day}\] Because their rate of growth is larger, the Hybrid A seedlings grow faster. in-context
