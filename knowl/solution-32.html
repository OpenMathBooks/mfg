Begin by applying the fifth law. \begin{align*} \require{\stackengine} \left(\frac{2x}{z^2}\right)^3 \amp = \frac{(2x)^3}{\left(z^2\right)^2}\amp\amp {\text{Apply the fourth law to the numerator }}{\text{and the third law to the denominator.}} \\ \amp = \frac{2^3x^3}{z^6}=\frac{8x^3}{z^6} \end{align*}
