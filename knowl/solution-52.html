SolutionA.86.1

Factor the denominators of each of the given fractions. \begin{equation*}x^2 − 1 = (x − 1) (x + 1)~~~\text{ and }~~~x^2 + x = x (x + 1)\end{equation*} The factor \((x − 1)\) occurs once in the first denominator, the factor \(x\) occurs once in the second denominator, and the factor \((x + 1)\) occurs once in each denominator. Therefore, we include in our LCD one copy of each of these factors. The LCD is \(x (x + 1) (x − 1)\).
