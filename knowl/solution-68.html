SolutionA.126.1

The box has six sides; we must find the area of each side and add them. The top and bottom of the box each have area \(lw\text{,}\) so together they contribute \(2lw\) to the surface area. The back and front of the box each have area \(lh\text{,}\) so they contribute \(2lh\) to the surface area. Finally, the left and right sides of the box each have area \(wh\text{,}\) so they add \(2wh\) to the surface area. Thus, the total surface area is \begin{equation*}S = 2lw + 2lh + 2wh\end{equation*}
