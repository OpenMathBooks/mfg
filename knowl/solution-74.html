Because \(v\) varies directly with \(t\), there is a positive constant \(k\) for which \begin{equation*}v = kt\end{equation*} Substitute \(v = 39.2\) when \(t = 4\) and solve for \(k\) to find \begin{align*} 39.2 \amp = k(4)\amp\amp \text{Divide both sides by 4.}\\ k = 9.8 \end{align*} Thus, \(v (t) = 9.8 t\).
