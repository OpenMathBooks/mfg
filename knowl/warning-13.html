CautionA.93

  1. In Example A.92a, note that \begin{equation*}\frac{1}{x}-\frac{1}{y}\ne\frac{1}{x-y} \end{equation*} For example, you can check that for \(x=2\) and \(y=3\text{,}\) \begin{equation*}\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{3}\ne\frac{1}{2-3}=-1 \end{equation*}

  2. In Example A.92b, note that \begin{equation*}\left(x^{-2}+y^{-2}\right)^{-1}\ne x^2+y^2\end{equation*}
