Caution1.69

Do not confuse the expression \(\frac{\log_{10}{7}}{\log_{10}{5}}\) with \(\log_{10}{\left(\frac{7}{5}\right)}\); they are not the same! Property (2) allows us to simplify \(\log{\left(\frac{x}{y}\right)}\), but not \(\frac{\log x}{\log y}\). We cannot rewrite \(\frac{\log_{10}{7}}{\log_{10}{5}}\), so we must evaluate it as \((\log 7)/(\log 5)\). You can check on your calculator that \begin{equation*}\frac{\log_{10}{7}}{\log_{10}{5}}\ne \log_{10}{\left(\frac{7}{5}\right)}= \log_{10}{1.4}\end{equation*}.
